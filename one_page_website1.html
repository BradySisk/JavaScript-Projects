    More Tips

    The most important points you need to know about darius is that his Q has a low cooldown, his Q does more damage if the enemy gets hit by the axe instead of the handle, his W in an auto attack reset and he gains movement speed when enemies have bleed stacks on them. When harassing the enemy, try to make sure you hit him with the outside of your Q, to do the most damage. This is fairly elementary. When you want to do the most damage possible (depends on enemy escape abilities), here is the general combo: Q from max range (1 stack) E the enemy towards you (still 1 stack) Use the minor knockup/CC from the E to land an auto attack, while using your W as an AA reset right after the AA damage lands, landing an auto beforehand also decreases W's cooldown. (3 stacks) You've gained 5% movementspeed at this point, since the enemy is bleeding and your opponent is slowed. This means you have time for another auto attack. (4 stacks) At this point your Q should be off cooldown and you can try to make the Q deal it's max damage by being further apart (5 stacks). Now that you have 5 stacks on the enemy, you can try to ult your opponent for the full damage, if you can kill him with it.
